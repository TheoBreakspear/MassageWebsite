About

I am an ITEC level 3 qualified massage therapist offering home visits in and around south Buckinghamshire. I also offer appointments at Breakspear Osteopathy and Pilates Clinic in Penn, where I work alongside the osteopathy team to provide massage for patients with musculoskeletal problems.

Treatments

Every massage is tailored to how you are feeling on the day, but many clients like to ask for one of the following treatment styles...

Deep-Tissue Massage

A stronger massage working deeply into the muscles to reduce tension, increase flexibility and improve posture. This is my most popular form of massage.

Holistic Massage

A gentler form of massage to help relax the muscles, aid lymphatic drainage and improve circulation. Perfect if you prefer a gentler massage.

De-stress Massage

A slow and flowing massage designed for total relaxation to help you unwind and recharge.
